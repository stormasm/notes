
### Royalty Purple Pod Bush Beans

- [royalty purple pod seeds](https://trueleafmarket.com/products/organic-royalty-purple-pod-bush-bean)

Royalty Purple Pod bush beans typically contain approximately 80 to 100 seeds per ounce. For planting, one ounce is usually sufficient to sow a short row, while a full pound is recommended for larger, more productive garden plots, commonly available at suppliers like True Leaf Market.

Average Seed Count: ~80-100 seeds/oz.

Specific Range: 84 to 95 seeds per ounce are frequently cited in Everwilde Farms and Victory Seed Company listings.

Weight Metric: Approximately 1,300–1,500 seeds per pound.


These heirloom, stringless beans are known for their dark purple pods, which turn green upon cooking, and their ability to germinate well in cold, wet soil.

Related Varieties
Be aware that similar purple bush beans, such as the Royal Burgundy, often have a similar count of roughly 87–110 seeds per ounce.

Yes, Royalty Purple Pod bush beans are excellent to eat; they are specifically classified as snap beans, meaning the entire pod and the immature seeds inside are meant to be consumed together.

The pods are stringless, crisp, and tender with a delicious, sweet flavor that is enjoyable both raw in salads and cooked.

While the pods are bright purple when raw, they turn green during cooking due to the breakdown of anthocyanins, though the taste remains high quality.

They are highly productive and easy to harvest, with the contrasting purple color making them easy to spot on the bush compared to traditional green varieties.
